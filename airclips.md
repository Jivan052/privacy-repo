# Airclips Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 28, 2026

## Overview

Airclips is a local-first Chrome extension that helps you capture copied
content, create notes, organize snippets, and transform content. Your
privacy is a core design principle.

## Information We Collect

Airclips **does not collect, transmit, sell, or share** your personal
information.

The extension stores only the content that **you explicitly create or
choose to save**, including: - Notes - Copied text and snippets - Tags
and categories - Extension preferences and settings

All of this data is stored **locally in your browser**.

## Local Storage

Airclips uses Chrome's local storage APIs to save your data on your
device. Your information is never uploaded to our servers because
Airclips does not operate any backend service.

## Clipboard Access

If you enable automatic clipboard capture or choose to import clipboard
content, Airclips temporarily reads clipboard data to save it locally.
Clipboard data is never transmitted to external servers.

## Website Access

Airclips requests access to webpages only to provide its core
functionality, such as capturing selected or copied content from pages
where you choose to use the extension. The extension does not monitor
your browsing history or collect website data for analytics or
advertising.

## Permissions Used

-   **Storage** -- Save notes, snippets, and preferences locally.
-   **Clipboard Read/Write** -- Capture or copy content when initiated
    by the user.
-   **Context Menus** -- Save selected text using the right-click menu.
-   **Side Panel** -- Display Airclips alongside webpages.
-   **Active Tab & Scripting** -- Capture selected content from the
    current webpage after user interaction.

## Third-Party Services

Airclips does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your data to any third party.

## Data Security

Because your information remains on your own device, you maintain full
control over your data. You may delete your notes and snippets at any
time from within the extension.

## Children's Privacy

Airclips is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does
not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the extension
or applicable laws. Any updates will be published with a revised
effective date.

## Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Airclips
developer through the Chrome Web Store listing or the project's official
repository or website.
